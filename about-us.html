About Celebriframe

Celebrating Life, One Frame at a Time

Our Story

Celebriframe was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a space where everyone can capture professional-quality photos of themselves, without the need for expensive equipment or professional photographers. We believe that every moment deserves to be celebrated, and every person deserves to feel confident and beautiful in their photos.

What started as a passion project has grown into the Philippines' premier self-photo studio. We've had the privilege of capturing thousands of precious moments—from intimate solo portraits to joyful family gatherings, from romantic couple sessions to milestone celebrations.

Our mission is simple: to empower people to tell their own stories through photography. We provide the tools, the space, and the creative environment. You bring the personality, the joy, and the authenticity. Together, we create memories that last a lifetime.

Meet the Founder

The Creative Mind Behind Celebriframe

Angel Morales

Founder & Creative Director

Angel Morales is a passionate photographer and entrepreneur with a vision to democratize professional photography. With years of experience in the photography industry, Angel recognized a gap in the market—people wanted beautiful photos but felt intimidated by traditional studio settings.

Driven by this insight, Angel created Celebriframe: a self-photo studio that combines professional-grade equipment with a welcoming, judgment-free environment. The result? Thousands of clients who have discovered their best selves through our studio.

Angel's philosophy is simple: "Everyone deserves to feel beautiful and confident in their photos. Our job is to provide the space and tools to make that happen."
